This persona is the key to improving facilities for people in need

He is a primary persona that knows the problem very well and that devoted his personal life to achieving a better city for blind people

He uses public transportation in our city daily and is aware of the real problems that this system encounters

He has interacted with both the app that the public transportation company of the city provides and deals with the problems in various bus stations every day.

His practical goals are achieving a better public transportation system for the people in need and creating a better version of the app together with the people in charge

On a personal plan he needs this new feautures because he is affected by this problem every day when he travels around the city

His business goals are representing the class of people that require his help and bringing their ideas in front of people capable of making the change
